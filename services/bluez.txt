package:     bluez
service:     bluetoothd
description:
  The BlueZ Bluetooth stack daemon. Manages Bluetooth hardware, device
  pairing and discovery, and Bluetooth profiles including audio (A2DP, HFP),
  HID (keyboards, mice), and serial devices. Exposes a D-Bus API used by
  desktop Bluetooth managers and by WirePlumber/PipeWire for Bluetooth audio.

  Requires dbus to be running. For Bluetooth audio to work, wireplumber must
  also be running as it manages the A2DP/HFP audio profiles.

  After install, the runit service is at /etc/sv/bluetoothd.
